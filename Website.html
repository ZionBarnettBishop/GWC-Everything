About Me!

I am 17 years old and I currently attend Kenwood Academy High School. I pride myself on being apart of many clubs and activites to add to my life experience and resume. I am originally from Chicago's south side, but at age 8, I moved to Vernon Hills, Il. Vernon Hills is a predominantely white town where I had to redefine my identity in an unfamiliar space. This experience helped to develop my character and outlook on life. My Sophomore year I transferred to Kenwood Academy in Hyde Park. Being surrounded by students that looked liked me and had similiar experiences gave me the confidence to be the best version of myself. After switching schools I began to excel academically and I dedicated myself to even more clubs and activities.
